At Prairie Wellness, our mission is to promote healthy living and empower individuals toward positive change. 

There are times in our lives when we could all benefit from support and having someone you can trust to help through difficult times. At Prairie Wellness, we believe that every person is entitled to live their best life and we want to be there with you every step of the way toward meeting your goals.  

Our dedicated staff of behavioral health clinicians is highly trained in many specialty areas to help identify effective, evidence-based strategies to help you cope with difficult situations. We offer individual various types of therapy for adults, children and adolescents. We can help you to create new habits and make life-long changes.


We are here for you when you need us. Please contact us today and take that first step toward wellness.
